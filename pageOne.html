Self-portait in a Straw Hat

by Louise Elisabeth Lebrun

The painting appears, after cleaning, to be an autograph replica of a picture, the origninal of which was painted in Brussels in 1782 in free imitation of Rubens's 'Chapeau de Paille', which LeBrun had seen in Antwerp. It was exhibited in Paris in 1782 at the Salon de la Correspondance. LeBrun's orginal is recorded in a private collection in France.
